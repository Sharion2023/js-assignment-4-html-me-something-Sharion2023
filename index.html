An Interesting Journey...

Where I thought I'd be as an adult:

If you had asked me fresh out of high school what I'd be doing with my life in 20 years, I'd have told you a much different
story than what I'm actually living today. I was always great at math and I loved physics, so engineering was supposedly my destiny. It took me a lot of classes and an internship to figure out that I just wasn't cut out for a cubicle! So, I ended that dream with an Associates's of Applied Science degree in Engineering. I pivoted and obtained an Associate's of Applied Science degreen in Respiratory Care. And little did I know, that was just the beginning of how differently things would shape up.
